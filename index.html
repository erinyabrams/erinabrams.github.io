Product Philosophy

Approach

I believe product management is the art of reducing ambiguity at scale. I lead with clarity, align cross-functional teams around measurable outcomes, and always advocate for thoughtful user experiences. With experience in both large organizations and startup skeleton crews, I'm equally energized by 0→1 systems design and continuous optimization - especially when it unlocks operational excellence or enables creativity across disciplines.
